A Bountiful Burger

Learn how to make succulent beef burgers with just four Ingredients. An easy recipe for perfect homemade patties

Ingredients

  • 1 small onion, diced
  • 500g good-quality beef mince
  • 1 egg
  • 1 tbsp vegetable oil
  • 4 burger buns
  • A shed load of cheese!

Steps

  1. Tip 500g beef mince into a bowl with 1 small diced onion and 1 egg, then mix
  2. Divide the mixture into four. Lightly wet your hands. Carefully roll the mixture into balls
  3. Set in the palm of your hand and gently squeeze down to flatten into patties about 3cm thick
  4. Make sure all burgers are the same thickness so that they will cook
  5. Fry those suckers up!
